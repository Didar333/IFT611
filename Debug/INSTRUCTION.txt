CONTRÔLE
-----------------------------
W - Accélérer
S - Decélérer
A - Move left
D - Move Right
SPACE - Fire

OTHER
-----------------------------
ESCAPE - Quit game
R - Debug mode

DEBUG
-----------------------------
T - Spawn player
Y - Spawn big asteroid
U - Spawn medium asteroid
I - Spawn small asteroid
O - Spawn big saucer (non fonctionnel)
P - Spawn small saucer (non fonctionnel)
